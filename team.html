THE GENERAL MANAGER


The General Manager oversees all aspects of the hotel operations including: guest relations, front desk, housekeeping, maintenance, finances, team building, and staff development.

The General Manager must possess strong communication skills, both verbal and written and demonstrate outstanding leadership.

The manager must be able to delegate responsibilities, organizecomplex projects, and establish priorities consistent with hotel objectives.
